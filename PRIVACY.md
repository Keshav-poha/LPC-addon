# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Animated Sprites Adobe Express Add-on ("Software") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We respect your privacy. **The Animated Sprites Add-on does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage metrics, or telemetry.** 

All character generation, rendering, and compositing processes occur entirely locally within your browser/client device. 

## 2. Third-Party Services and Network Requests
In order to function, the Software dynamically fetches public sprite images from a third-party Content Delivery Network (CDN):
- **GitHub Raw Files:** The Add-on downloads open-source pixel art assets directly from the public Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC) repository hosted on GitHub (`raw.githubusercontent.com`).
- When these images are fetched, your browser sends standard HTTP requests to GitHub's servers, which may include your IP address and user-agent string as inherently required by internet protocols. 
- We do not have access to, nor do we store or process, any information logged by GitHub during these asset requests. For more information, please refer to [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

## 3. Data Storage
The Software relies on the temporary memory of your active Adobe Express session. No cookies, local storage, or persistent tracking mechanisms are used by this Add-on to monitor your activity.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because we do not collect any personal information, our Software is inherently safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our project repository.
